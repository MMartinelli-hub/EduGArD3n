Project Objectives

Edu‑GArD3n aims to enhance education in additive manufacturing by integrating sustainability and innovative teaching methods. The project provides students with modern tools, green approaches and practical learing activities to support the development of responsible and future-oriented additive manufacturing skills.

Integrate Sustainability Principles

Integrate sustainability principles into additive manufacturing education, ensuring students develop eco-conscious approaches to modern production technologies.

Provide Innovative Learning Resources

Provide students with innovative learning resources, including interactive e-learning platforms and virtual laboratories for hands-on experience.

Promote Greener Production Practices

Promote the adoption of greener and more efficient production practices, empowering the next generation to drive sustainable innovation in manufacturing.
